    • TEDx Afariwaa

    Unleashing Ideas, A prerequisite for change!

    TEDx Afariwaa creates a new platform for the country's sharpest thinkers and visionaries to inspire the youth to be agents of change and development. TEDx Afariwaa is an exciting, insightful and thought-provoking, with talks and discussions which will stir up the “change” agenda in the community. In the spirit of ideas spreading, TEDx Afariwaa has its radar on issues that will encourage attendees to explore ideas, push them to be innovative and collaborate to champion the change and development.

    TALKS

    Diversified thought-provoking and inspiring talks by our speakers to challenge you to be an agent of change and development

    PERFORMANCES

    Intriguing performances of art and culture to illuminate your TEDx experience

    PLEDGES

    Vouching to make a positive impact in developing yourself and your community and being an advocate for change and development
