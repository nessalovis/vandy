Meet the people that make it happen

Charlse Mensah

Curator

Charles Mensah is a social entrepreneur and has some level of experience in digital media and social innovation. He was part of the selected delegates from Ghana, for the Global Media Forum 2015 in Bonn, Germany. He currently work as the Head of Media in 1 Billion Africa, a high impact non-profit organisation which empowers Africa’s youth to turn problems into projects in their communities. He co-leads Sikasem in driving the mission forward.

Faithful Akpaloo

Co-Curator

Faithful Akpaloo is a young man who is passionate about good leadership and community empowerment. He is a graduate of Valley View University in Business Administration. He has had stints with Tema Oil Refinery as an Accounts Assistant. He has held various leadership positions in numerous youth groups who are committed to human capital development. Faithful is currently on of the leads in the Accounting a Finance team at Alive Gas Limited.

Deputy Program Manager

Isaac Quainoo is a banking professional and co-founder of Sikasem, a social enterprise that is dedicated to improving financial literacy among people in Ghana’s informal sector. He has worked with Tema Oil Refinery as a Finance Intern and SG-SSB as Relationship Officer. He Student Member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers, Ghana. He is an avid learner and a staunch believer of Africa’s potentials as well as community development.
